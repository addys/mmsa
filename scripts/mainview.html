Introduction

The Microsoft Ventures Accelerator runs a customized program which we tailor to the specific needs of your company. This assessment is a useful tool for uncovering those needs, and determining which areas of focus will maximize the benefit your company derives from the Accelerator program.

Instructions

Step 1 – Rate Yourself

This document enables you to rate yourself against many different dimensions. Each dimension includes a table with several descriptions, numbered in ascending order. Choose the highest-valued description which fully matches your company.

Step 2 – Prioritize

This assessment helps to facilitate a productive discussion of your goals and challenges. The MVA team will work with you to refine (or define) the business, product and technical priorities to focus on.

Step 3 – Make a plan

Together we'll build a work plan to help you reach your goals. Given your plan, the MVA team can hook you up to the right resources (such as legal/financial/marketing/technical guidance and 1:1 mentor meetings).
